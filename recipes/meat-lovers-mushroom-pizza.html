Description

A pizza perfectly baked with toppings of chunks of meat and mushrooms.

This delightful meal is an ATK boosting dish, increasing all party members' ATK by 272 and CRIT Rate by 8% for 300s.

A Note: This was a limited time event food recipe and can no longer be obtained.

  • 3 Mushroom
  • 2 Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather raw materials such as raw meat, wheat, mushrooms, and milk
  2. Prepare the wheat into flour
  3. Prepare the milk into cheese
  4. Cook Meat Lover's Mushroom Pizza
