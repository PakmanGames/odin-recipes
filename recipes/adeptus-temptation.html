Adeptus' Temptation

Description

A complex legendary dish of Liyue Cuisine.

This scared dish is of the ATK boosting type dishes, it increases all party members' ATK by 316 and CRIT Rate by 10% for 300s.

Ingredients

  • 4 Ham
  • 3 Crab
  • 3 Shrimp Meat
  • 3 Matsutake

Steps

  1. Gather raw materials such as raw meat, salt, crab, shrimp meat, matsutake
  2. Prepare the raw meat and salt into ham
  3. Unlock the recipe from the luxurious chest above Qingyun Peak
  4. Cook Adeptus' Temptation
