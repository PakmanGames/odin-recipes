Sticky Honey Roast Recipe

Description

A meat dish paired with carrots and harmonized with the glazed sweet sauce.

This is an adventurer type dish that will decrease all party members' stamina consumption by 20% for 900s.

Ingredients

  • 2 Raw Meat
  • 2 Carrot
  • 2 Sugar

Steps

  1. Gather raw materials such as raw meat, carrots, and sweet flowers
  2. Prepare the sweet flowers by turning them into sugar
  3. Complete the quest: "Sara's Worries" to unlock the recipe
  4. Cook Stick Honey Roast
